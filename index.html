From Ghana With Love

Naa-Wuni Vela was born in the heart of Ghana's shea belt, where ancient traditions meet modern quality standards. Our name means "God is good" in the local dialect, reflecting our commitment to sharing nature's finest with the world.

2020

The Beginning

Founded by Stephen Dobia with a vision to empower local women and share Ghana's premium shea butter with the world.

2021

First Prototype

The shear was birthed. This is where the theoretical knowdledge was brought to life in a physical product.
