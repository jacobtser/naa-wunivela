From Ghana With Love

Stephen Andrews DOBIA founded the Naa-Wuni Vela (God is Good) Shea Empowering Project to empower rural women in the Bunkpurugu-Nakpanduri District, North-East Region, Ghana through sustainable shea butter production and agroforestry practices guided by a mission to create economic opportunities and restore dignity through work, the project equips women with practical skills, production resources, and market access to transform shea processing into a reliable source of income. Driven by a vision of self-reliant and resilient communities, Naa-Wuni Vela Shea Empowering Project combines social impact with enterprise development to promote long-term sustainability. Through women-led leadership and environmentally conscious practices, the project strengthens a locally rooted shea value chain that creates economic opportunities, uplifts communities, and fosters inclusive development.
